Clash Of Clans Tips For Beginners

· DO NO RUSH      {Rushing is a very common mistake. Normally a new player thinks that upgrading TownHall to the max is everything Ideally, you should be maxing out every building before upgrading your town hall. Your first priority should be your offensive structures, which are the Clan Castle for troops and the laboratory for upgrades. You can follow up with the army camps to increase the capacity of your army, followed by the strongest defenses.}

· SAVE GEMS      {Gem is very important. When getting assisted by the lady villager, it will tell you to use gems to finish the upgrading process fast. You should not use your gems for useless things. In the future, you will need a lot of gems to buy builders. So, start saving your gems now.}

· Utilise the Practice Mode      {The Practice Mode has been added in an update a few years ago, and players can use it to try out and discover new army compositions for attacks. You will eventually realize that you need to use tanks in your army which will soak up the damage from the enemy defenses while your other troops attack them.}

· Always Keep The Laboratory Busy      {You might have a lot of builders but only have a laboratory. Upgrading your troops is essential if you want to keep up with your Town Hall Level. Whenever you have a Town Hall upgrade, make sure that the Laboratory is almost done as well. You can set an upgrade to go in the lab, and immediately upgrade the lab itself to the next level to save some time.}

· Walls are actually worth it      { Walls might seem really, really expensive once you get to the higher levels, but it’s necessary for a good defense. A base that has weak walls leaves its structures exposed. The center of your base can be easily reached with low-level troops, so to ensure that it doesn’t happen you should max them out.}

Some Of the Upgrading Priorities That We Recomend:

· Heros

· Defence

· Troops

· Army Camps

· Walls

· Laboratory

· Spell Factory

· Clan Castle
